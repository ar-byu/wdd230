The York City Commerce site aims to create a secure place for business owners in York city and all over York County to network and expand their businesses. We aim to provide an easily accessible space to run promotions for fundraising for projects to strengthen our community. We also aim to provide advertising for local businesses, as many people will come here to use this site as an authority on where to shop. We plan on being a business partner to all local small businesses in York City and the surrounding areas.
