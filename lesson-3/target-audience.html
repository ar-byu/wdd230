There are two target audiences for this site. The first target audience for this site is small business owners in the York City area. These people will usually be young adults to middle aged. They will be from many income brackets, depending on where on their business-owning journey they are. They will have a variety of family statuses. They will have at least a high school level of education, if not a college level. These visitors will likely access this site from both desktop and mobile.

The second target audience is community members looking to either fundraise or lobby, or researching businesses to shop at. These visitors will likely be young adults to elderly. They will be from all income brackets and have a variety of family statuses. They will have a variety of education levels. These visitors will also access this site from both desktop and mobile, with a likely preference for mobile.

Some purposes for accessing this site are:

  • How do I advertise my business locally?
  • What fundraisers are going on right now?
  • Are there other businesses nearby that I can partner with?
  • What local businesses am I interested in visiting?
  • Where can I start a fundraiser?
