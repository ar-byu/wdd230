Color Scheme
Typography

The color scheme I will be using is made up of colors from the site's logo, as well as some neutral colors. These colors are:

  • Blue
  • Green
  • Light grey
  • White

I've chosen these colors because this color scheme is aesthetically pleasing, but there is also an emotional appeal to these colors. Blue promotes reassurance and relability, and green promotes peace and security.
