White Space

Domino's Pizza

dominos.com

White space is using an appropriate amount of padding and margin in between elements to keep a page from looking cluttered. I think that this example uses white space appropriately by giving a good amount of neutral space in between the navigation bar and each of the features on the main screen.

Contrast

Zelda Dungeon

zeldadungeon.net

Contrast is using color and sizing to draw a viewer's eye to specific parts of a page. I think that this example uses contrast appropriately by using eye-catching colors for its headers, as well as appropriate font sizing for text.

Rule of Thirds

Canva

canva.com

The rule of thirds is an artistic and design principle that divides a canvas, or in this case a webpage, into sections of three. This keeps it aesthetically pleasing and organized. I believe that this example uses the rule of thirds well by giving roughly equal space to each of the three main areas of the page.
