About Our Hotels

Temple Inn and Suites strives to provide affordable, quality lodging for both temple patrons and temple missionaries in the northeastern area of the United States.

Our hotels provide various services to guests, such as free transportation to temples, childcare, and a family history center. More information about hotel services can be found on our Services page.
