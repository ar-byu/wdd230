General Hotel Services

Temple Inn and Suites is proud to have a wide array of services and amenities for guests.

Specialized Services

  • Free access to changing rooms and bathrooms for temple patrons (whether or not you're staying at the hotel)
  • Low-cost childcare for children of hotel guests, starting at $10/hour
  • Long-term accomodations for temple missionaries. More information below
  • Temple history themed reception and sitting area
  • A wedding reception hall. More information below
  • Family history center that support FamilySearch and ordinance work

Free Amenities

  • Continental breakfast from 6:00 AM to 10:00 AM
  • Exercise room
  • Indoor pool
  • High-speed WiFi
  • Smart TVs
  • Parking
  • Temple shuttle
  • Family history consulation
  • Family ordinance card printing

Wedding Reception Services

Temple Inn and Suites hotels have a wedding reception hall* that can be reserved by contacting our specialized wedding department.

Our wedding reception services are:

  • Low-cost reception planning
  • Low-cost decorating service
  • Low-cost catering provided by the hotel (menu available upon request)
  • Optional: Kitchen access for food preparation and serving
  • Optional: Hotel-provided setup and takedown

To book a wedding reception hall, please e-mail RECEPTION@templeinn.com

Temple Missionary Services

Temple Inn and Suites offers long-term lodging for temple missionaries looking to stay close to their assigned temple. Our missionary suites include:

  • Kitchenette or full kitchen for food preparation
  • Housekeeping two days per week
  • Living area with TV
  • Walk-in closet
